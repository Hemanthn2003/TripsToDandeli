Water activities description

 

  • Boating.

    It is a water activity where 6-8 members are getting Safari from raft boat with a guide upto 2 kilometers.

  • Kayaking.

    It is a water activity in which a single person can Safari a kayaking boat within the radius of resort.

  • Rafting.

    It is a water activity where 6-8 members are Rafting With a guide upto 1.2 kilometers(6 and 9 kilometer Rafting is available on prebooking).

  • Zippline.

    Single person is been made to cross river by the help of Zippline and carried back to the initial point by the carrying boat.

  • Zorbing

    Single person is been made to cross river by the help of Zippline and carried back to the initial point by the carrying boat. 3-4 person are allowed to roll on zorbing baloon with Life jacket equipped for the guest(Only for person with weight less than 68kg).

  • Free Swimming.

    Single person is been made to cross river by the help of Zippline and carried back to the initial point by the carrying boat. Guests are equipped by life jacket an let to swimm freely within the radius of resort in the Kali river.
