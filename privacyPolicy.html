Privacy Policy

Your privacy, safety, and trust are our priority at Trips to Dandeli.

Our Privacy Commitment

At Trips to Dandeli, we value the privacy of every guest and visitor. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we ensure it remains safe and secure.

  • Information We Collect: Basic contact details when you voluntarily contact us.
  • On-Site Guest Verification: Government ID requested on arrival for verification.
  • Purpose of Data Collection: Manage bookings, provide assistance, and maintain records.
  • Data Sharing: Only with authorities if required by law.
  • Data Retention: Securely stored as long as necessary.
  • Cookies and Analytics: For website improvement and Google Ads tracking.
  • Your Rights: Guests can request correction or removal of contact details anytime.
  • Policy Updates: Updated periodically for compliance improvements.

We are committed to transparency and integrity, ensuring every guest feels safe and respected.

Thank you for trusting Trips to Dandeli.
